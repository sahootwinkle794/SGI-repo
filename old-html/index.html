Gamma irradiation is a highly reliable, internationally accepted sterilization and decontamination process that uses controlled doses of gamma rays to eliminate microorganisms, pathogens, insects, and other biological contaminants from products. It is a cold, chemical-free, residue-free technology ideal for industries such as food, pharmaceuticals, agriculture, spices, and medical devices.

At Sukshma Gamma LLP, we use Cobalt-60 (^60Co), a proven gamma radiation source recognized by global bodies for safe and effective sterilization like

  • WHO

  • FAO

  • IAEA

  • FSSAI
